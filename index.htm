Chantra Finance is an algorithmic stablecoin protocol bringing sustainability to the Binance ecosystem through chantra-backed treasury reserves.
Inspired by Tomb Finance, the Chantra protocol uses seigniorage mechanisms to ensure price stability and to enable bonding $CTA credits, permanently locking CTA in its CTA reserve.
